How it works

Built for the city that never rests.

Uly Dala fuses real-time sensor data, historical traffic patterns, and large language models to produce actionable forecasts — not just visualisations.

Open the live map →
01

Data ingestion

75+ air quality stations and 82 road segments feed live readings — congestion levels, PM2.5, temperature, wind speed — into the platform every hour.

02

AI-driven prediction

A predictive model trained on Almaty's rush-hour rhythms, weekday/weekend cycles, Friday PM surges, and weather impact generates 72-hour forecasts.

03

Gemini synthesises insights

Google Gemini reads the forecast context and writes natural-language advisories for citizens — and strategic action items for the Akimat — in real time.
